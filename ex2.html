Shafali Verma - the 17-year-old batting sensation - has earned her maiden call up to the Indian ODI and the Test squad for the upcoming all format tour to England. The selectors announced two squads, an 18-member common one for the one-off Test and the three-match ODI series, set to be led by Mithali Raj, along with a 17-member squad for the T20Is, to be led by Harmanpreet Kaur

Shikha Pandey and Tanya Bhatia, who were dropped for the ODI and the T20I series against South Africa at home, too have been called back. Uncapped wicket-keeper, Indrani Roy, finds herself in both squads along, while left arm spinner, Rajeshwari Gayakwad, who hasn't recovered from a bout of Covid-19, has been left out.
